💡 Understanding Your Targets

Your calorie and protein targets are calculated using the Mifflin-St Jeor equation, adjusted for your goal. Water intake is based on your weight (35ml/kg).

🏋️ Protein Guide

  • 🟢 General Health: 0.8–1.0 g/kg
  • 🟡 Active / Fat Loss: 1.6–1.8 g/kg
  • 🔴 Muscle Gain: 1.8–2.2 g/kg

💧 Water Guide

  • 🧊 Minimum: 30 ml/kg
  • 🏃 Active / Hot climate: 35–40 ml/kg
